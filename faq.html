Frequently Asked Questions

What is AniSuki?
AniSuki is a fun fan-made anime platform for browsing and watching your favorite shows.
Is AniSuki free to use?
Yes! AniSuki is completely free and created for entertainment and learning purposes.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required to browse or watch content on AniSuki.
Can I suggest an anime to be added?
Yes, you can contact us via social media or Discord to suggest new titles.
Where does the content come from?
All media is embedded from official sources or used for demonstration purposes only.
